TCHS SciOly was founded in 1998. Every year since 2013, we have qualified for the State tournament, and since 2021, we have placed at States almost every year. In 2023, we hosted BirdSo, which remains one of the largest in-person invitationals in SciOly history at 77 competing teams. Starting that same year, we have hosted the Div A Los Angeles Regional tournament, with 44-51 elementary schools in attendance. Last year, during the 2025 season, we added a 2nd team, which allowed us to show one of our best performances ever. The addition of our 2nd team has strongly impacted our performance and helped create the SciOly family we love.
