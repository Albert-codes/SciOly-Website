History

TCHS SciOly was founded in 1998. Every year since 2013, we have qualified for the State tournament, and since 2021, we have placed at States every year except 2024. In 2023, we hosted BirdSo, which remains as one of the largest in-person invitationals in SciOly history at 77 competeting teams. Starting the same year, we have hosted the Div A Los Angeles Regional tournament, with 44-51 elementary schools in attendance. Last year, during the 2025 season, we added a 2nd team, which allowed us to show one of our best performaces in our history. The additon of Team 2 has strongly impacted our performance and helped create SciOly family we love.
