Banquet

After a long exhilarating season, our team held our banquet at Huntington beach on May 26th, 2025, where we held out near a campsite. Our members built sand castles, went boogie boarding, and interacted in many team activities, like a test between the 3 captains on the events and members of the team. The captains for 2025 elected the captains for 2026, which was revealed at the end of the night to be Rylan Chau(11), Mathew Kong(12), and Ethan Deng(12)!

States

At California Institute of Technology, TCHS SciOly(we?) competeted in the Socal State Competition on May 12th, 2025. Before award ceremony, our members took a deserved break, watching shows, enjoying delicious food brought by parents, and playing football with each other. Our team medaled in 12 different events placing 5th place overall!

Event Medals: 3rd Anatomy & Physiology, 6th Disease Detectives, 3rd Dynamic Planet, 2nd Entomology, 2nd Experimental Design, 6th Forensics, 4th Geologic Mapping, 2nd Material Science, 5th Microbe Mission, 3rd Towers, 4th Wind Power, 3rd Botany(Trial)

Regionals

Both Temple City teams competed in the Pasendena City College(PCC) Regional on April 28th, 2025, where we took our first step towards States. Our members conversed in many activites during breaks, exploring the campus, eating, and even gambling with participation medals as a currency! Our teams collectively gained 39 medals, many of which consisted of 1st and 2nd place. Team A placed 1st overall and Team B placed 2nd overall, allowing us to advance towards States.

Event Medals:
Team A - 1st Air Trajectory, 2nd Anatomy & Physiology, 2nd Astronomy, 1st Bungee Drop, 4th Chemistry Lab, 1st Codebusters, 2nd Disease Detectives, 2nd Dynamic Planet, 1st Ecology, 3rd Electric Vehicle, 1st Entomology, 1st Experimental Design, 1st Forensics, 2nd Fossils, 1st Geologic Mapping, 2nd Helicopter, 2nd Material Science, 1st Microbe Mission, 3rd Optics, 1st Robot Tour, 1st Towers, 3rd Wind Power
Team B - 1st Anatomy & Physiology, 3rd Astronomy, 2nd Bungee Drop, 2nd Chemistry Lab, 3rd Codebusters, 3rd Disease Detectives, 1st Dynamic Planet, 1st Electric Vehicle, 3rd Experimental Design, 4th Forensics, 1st Fossils, 2nd Geologic Mapping, 3rd Helicopter, 3rd Material Science, 2nd Microbe Mission, 2nd Towers, 2nd Wind Power
