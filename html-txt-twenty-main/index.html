About Us

Welcome to the TCHS SciOly Website! Science Olympiad is a academic, track meet-style competition focused around 23 STEM events, such as build events, lab events, test events, or even a mixture of both! Science Olympiad consists of teams of 15 students, who partner in various events where top teams in events may potentially win medals. Science Olympiad helps promote the curiosities and education of science, and pushes for students to work together and cooperate.

TCHS SciOly is a club founded in 1998, competing in Division C as a primarily student-run club. We have two teams consisting of 15 students each, and throughout the year, our teams join invitational, regional, and state competitions to compete with other schools. During competitions, partners go to their events through blocks, just like a school schedule. When we aren't testing, we are eating and enjoying our teammates company. As a team, we strongly value the family spirit, and often have team bondings, team dinners, and celebrations! We are amongst one of the most popular academic clubs at our school and we are highly competetive, with consistently high rankings at our regional and state tournament. We expect our students to prioritize TCHS SciOly and attend every competition.
