Experience

Through our team, our members obtain valuable experience in topics they want to pursue. Studying for events allows our members to gain the valuable hands-on experience of engineering or lab work that you wouldn't be able to find in school.



Competitions

With our teams, we attend 6-8 virtual and in-person competitions. Many of these competitions are hosted on university campuses, including USC, Berkeley, and Caltech. At these competitions, we may receive tours of the campus, providing our members with valuable insight into college life. During competition day, there is endless fun and opportunities to win medals in your events!



Curiosity

Science Olympiad offers a vast array of topics ranging from engineering to labs to research. These events provide abundant subjects for our members to study. With 23 fascinating events, anyone interested in STEM can find a place to participate in TCHS SciOly!



Family

From TCHS SciOly, our members don't just gain valuable experiences, but also form many friends and connections who will accompany each other all throughout high school! On our teams, we wish the best for everyone and our team's morale is invaluable!
